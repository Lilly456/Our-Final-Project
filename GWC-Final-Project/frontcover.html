Our Mission

Accessible Educational Resources

Education

Our goal is to provide a resourceful site for young students in high school. To make the school experience memorable and the college process effortless, we gathered all the essential resources in one site.

Future

To ensure all students are given an opportunity to continue their education in college, our site contains lists of programs and internships to build your resume and scholarships for those in need.

Action

As the next generation of leaders in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, we hope to ensure every student the appropriate essentials to thrive and help others on their journey.

Passion

Our own confusion and frustration in figuring out possible futures is not uncommon so we realized that with our knowledge on coding and the importance of education we can make a difference where we care most.
